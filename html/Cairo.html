Cairo Food Guide

Texas BBQ

Houston is renowned for its delicious Texas-style barbecue. Enjoy mouthwatering brisket, ribs, and sausage, slow-cooked to perfection and served with classic sides like coleslaw and baked beans.

Crawfish Boil

Experience the vibrant flavors of Louisiana with a traditional crawfish boil. In Houston, you'll find numerous restaurants serving up this spicy and savory seafood feast, often accompanied by corn, potatoes, and sausage.

Tex-Mex Cuisine

Indulge in the fusion of Texan and Mexican flavors with Houston's diverse Tex-Mex cuisine. From sizzling fajitas to cheesy enchiladas, you'll find a wide range of delicious dishes influenced by both cultures.
