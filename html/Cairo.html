Felfela

A culinary icon in Cairo since 1959, Felfela serves authentic Egyptian and Middle Eastern cuisine. Its diverse menu caters to all tastes, featuring savory mezze platters and succulent grilled meats.

Nestled in Cairo's bustling atmosphere, Felfela offers a warm welcome to diners seeking the rich flavors of Egyptian cuisine. Indulge in traditional desserts like basbousa and baklava for a satisfying end to your meal.

Located centrally, Felfela promises not just a meal, but a culinary journey through Egypt's gastronomic heritage. With its quality ingredients and warm hospitality, it remains a beloved destination for authentic Egyptian dining.

Naguib Mahfouz Cafe and Restaurant

Nestled in Cairo's Khan El-Khalili district, Naguib Mahfouz Cafe and Restaurant offers a unique blend of traditional Egyptian cuisine and vibrant atmosphere. Diners enjoy classics like kofta and falafel alongside flavorful salads and grilled meats.

The restaurant also boasts a tempting selection of desserts, including creamy Om Ali and sweet baklava. Set against the bustling Khan El-Khalili market, it provides a cultural journey through Egyptian heritage.

Naguib Mahfouz Cafe and Restaurant, with its warm hospitality and historic ambiance, is a beloved culinary destination in Cairo. Its traditional decor and vibrant atmosphere offer an immersive dining experience in the heart of the bustling Khan El-Khalili market. Whether dining with family or friends, guests enjoy delicious food and a sense of tradition that makes every visit memorable.
